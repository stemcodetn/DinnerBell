DinnerBell

Uniting us over what we all share in common: food.

Vision

DinnerBell is a progressive web application designed to create community over shared meals. You will join or create events in your area, with the hope of meeting new people and forming lasting bonds.

Location

To personalize your experience, we use your location data to pinpoint and plot events near you. However, we realize the need for privacy. That is why no one will see your location accept us.

Ionic

To create this app, we used the Ionic Framework, a mobile-first development platform built off of Angular. It natively implements the techniques and methods of Material Design to create a beautiful and simplistic user experience.

Mapbox

The most important visual part of our application, the map, was built with Mapbox, a free vector map platform that allows for a large amount of customizability.

Firebase

For our event storage and user authentication, we utilized Google's Firebase program, a No-SQL server platform that allows for simple account setup, realtime storage, and powerful tools to keep our app experience fresh and perfect.
